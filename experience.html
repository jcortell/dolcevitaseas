On-board Cuisine

Private Chef will prepare our meals on-board. Most special diets can be accommodated.

DINING

Restaurants

Dining out will be an experience to remember. Restaurants are selected based on the superb quality of authentic cuisine and unique setting.

DINING

Local Specialties

The yacht will be stocked with seasonal local produce, artisanal gelato, regional treats and wines. All cuisine will be fresh and locally-sourced.

ON-BOARD ACTIVITIES

Food

Observe our Chef prepare the catch-of-the-day in the open kitchen. Learn how to make classic regional dishes like gazpacho that you can easily make at home.

ON-BOARD ACTIVITIES

Tastings

We’ll invite experts to host tastings (wine, jamon, and cheese) with an opportunity to ship favorites back home.

ON-BOARD ACTIVITIES

Enrichment

Seminars can be arranged based on your interests. Local instructors will be brought on-board.

ON-BOARD ACTIVITIES

Entertainment

Local performers will join us in the evening. Movies filmed on location will be screened in the Salon.

ON-BOARD ACTIVITIES

Water toys

Jet skis (personal watercraft), Inflatables, Waterslides, Snorkeling or Scuba diving gear, Waterskiing, kayaks, seabob...

SHORE EXPERIENCES
