STEPHANIE SERINO

Known in the industry as the Cruise Curator

Stephanie is a know and respected voice in the Luxury Cruise industry. Her views, opinion and advice have been featured in: The New York Times, Condé Nast Traveler, Forbes, The Wall Street Journal Market Watch, Cruise Week, Travel Weekly, Travel+Leisure, Travel Pulse, USA Today, NBC News, CNBC...

But there's a lot more behind Stephanie. Her agency (Serino Travel Services) belongs to one of the largest travel conglomerates in the world: the NY based Tzell - Internova Travel Group. Companies like American Express Travel are also part of this group. This gives her unmatched negotiation power with vendors around the world.
