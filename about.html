About us

Stephanie & Jorge

You're in good hands

Knowledge, experience, dedication, and professionalism. With love from our family to yours.

Combining decades of Luxury Cruise industry experience with a vetted portfolio of luxury yacht charters, skippers, crew, guides, drivers, and vendors. Topping it off with deep knowledge of the Western Mediterranean, makes Dolce Vita Seas the perfect company to curate a wonderful Private Yacht Cruise for you and your loved ones.

STEPHANIE SERINO, Chief Experience Officer - CXO

Cruise Curator

An Italian-American who moved from New York City to Spain, Stephanie is known as the Cruise Curator. She has been in the Luxury Cruise industry since the turn of the century, traveling on and touring enough ships to assemble a small fleet. Passionate bon vivant with infectious enthusiasm for the Mediterranean lifestyle.

JORGE CORTELL, President & CEO

Born Mediterranean

Born in Spain, and having spent many childhood summers in Ibiza, Jorge is a former Professional Basketball Player, University Lecturer, Tech Entrepreneur, and European Union Expert on Philanthropy and Social Investment. Jorge has traveled extensively throughout the Mediterranean and he'll be happy to share a way of life with you.
